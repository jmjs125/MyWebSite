Fiverr

On Fiverr, I offer a professional QA Testing Gig where I will playtest your game and provide two detailed reports. The first report focuses on the overall experience of your game. I break this down into key areas such as: Visual Design Gameplay and Mechanics Audio Quality Additional Feedback (based on any specific aspects you'd like me to focus on) The second report covers bug tracking. For every bug I encounter, I’ll provide a separate, clear, and structured bug report that includes steps to reproduce, severity level, and suggestions (if applicable). Whether you're looking for general feedback or detailed QA insights, I’m here to help you improve your game before release.
